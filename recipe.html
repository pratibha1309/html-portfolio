Pasta Recipe

Ingredient

  • 1 red onion
  • 2 red peppers
  • 120 g bacon
  • 1 can (450 g) tomatoes
  • 1 cup water
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Oregeno
  • 50 g pasta per person

Method

  1. Cut the onion , red pepper and bacon into small pieces
  2. heat some olive oil in pan and fry the onion, red pepper and bacon
  3. Add oregano, Garlic, tomatoes and water and cook for 20 min
  4. cook th eoasta in a big pan of boiling water
  5. Serve the past with sauce and enjoy!
